
Privacy Policy for Chrome Extension "Fbff"
Effective Date: February 10, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Kridha Inc ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our Chrome extension "Fbff" ("Extension").

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from you when you use the Extension:

Email Address: Through Google OAuth Login, we collect your email address. This is used to uniquely identify you and provide personalized recommendations.
Website URLs and Product Information: When you browse shopping websites, the Extension collects the URLs of the websites you visit and information about the products you view, such as product names, prices, and images. This information is used to generate personalized recommendations for you.
3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

To authenticate you and provide personalized recommendations: Your email address is used to identify you as a unique user and to associate your browsing activity with your user profile. Your browsing activity, including website URLs and product information, is used to generate personalized recommendations for you based on your interests and shopping habits.
To improve the Extension: We use your information to improve the accuracy and relevance of our recommendations and to develop new features for the Extension.
To comply with legal and regulatory requirements: We may use and disclose your information as necessary to comply with applicable laws and regulations.
4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with any third-party entities without your consent. However, we may share non-personal information, such as aggregated browsing data, with third-party analytics providers to help us understand how users interact with the Extension and improve our services.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use industry-standard security measures, such as encryption and secure storage, to protect your personal information.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide you with the services offered by the Extension and to comply with applicable laws and regulations. You can request that we delete your information at any time by contacting us through the methods described in the "Contact Us" section below.

7. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your information:

Access your information: You can access your information by contacting us through the methods described in the "Contact Us" section below.
Correct your information: You can correct your information by contacting us through the methods described in the "Contact Us" section below.
Delete your information: You can request that we delete your information by contacting us through the methods described in the "Contact Us" section below.
Withdraw your consent: You can withdraw your consent to our collection and use of your information at any time by uninstalling the Extension.
8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
